Retail & Supply Chain Analytics

A Power BI dashboard analyzing $2M in sales across 5,009 orders for a North American retail chain. This analysis pinpointed key profit drivers, exposed loss-making segments in the supply chain, and evaluated logistics efficiency to provide actionable optimization strategies.

Key Insights Delivered:

  • Technology category was both the #1 profit driver AND the primary source of losses
  • Identified the Consumer segment as most profitable ($219K), outperforming Corporate
  • Central region identified as a "loss leader" despite West region leading in sales volume

Customer Behavior & RFM Analysis

An Excel-based RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis that segmented customers into four distinct behavioral groups. This strategic analysis validated the 80/20 rule and provided a targeted marketing framework to improve retention and customer lifetime value.

Key Insights Delivered:

  • Validated 80/20 rule: 20% of customers (High-Value Loyalists) generated 80% of revenue
  • Identified "At-Risk Spenders" segment with high historical value but declining engagement
  • Created tailored marketing strategies for each of the four customer segments
